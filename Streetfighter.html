Kawasaki Ninja H2 / H2R

The street-legal H2 produces roughly 200–210 hp, while the track-only H2R, featuring carbon fiber wings and extensive weight-saving, delivers an intense ~310–360 hp, designed for maximum acceleration and high-speed, closed-course performance.

Kawasaki ZX-14R (ZZR1400)

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-14R (ZZR1400) is a legendary high-performance sport bike, often referred to as the "King of the Quarter Mile". Powered by a 1,441cc inline-four engine, it produces over 200 horsepower, capable of 0–60 mph in roughly 2.5 seconds.
